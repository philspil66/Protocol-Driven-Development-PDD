# Who This Is For

This work is primarily for people who have responsibility for software that must keep working.

That includes:

- experienced developers maintaining long-lived systems
- technical leads and architects
- engineers uneasy about AI touching production code
- students thinking about how to develop durable skills in an AI-shaped industry

You do not need to agree with the ideas here to find value in them.
You do need to care about change, risk, and long-term consequences.

This is not written for people looking for a new framework to adopt wholesale.
It is written for people who recognise that something fundamental is shifting and want language and tools to think clearly about it.
